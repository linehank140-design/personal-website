Pet Industry: What Changed in the Last Decade

  • Premiumization: consumers trading up to higher quality nutrition and functional products.
  • Humanization: pets treated like family → more spend on wellness and specialty care.
  • eCommerce + Autoship: repeat delivery became a core growth engine for brands and retailers.
  • Retail media: sponsored search + onsite placement became critical to visibility.
  • Data-driven shelf: content, availability, defects, and ratings became performance levers.
  • Vet + online pharmacy: growing integration of clinics, services, and digital fulfillment.
